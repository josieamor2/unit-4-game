Crystal Collector

Rules


Goal of the game is to match the random number displayed by clicking the crystals.


Each crystal is designated a random number that you will have to figure out. As you click on them the score will need to equal the random number. If the number goes over what the random number equals, you lose.


Each time you win or lose, a new random number will be assigned. Along with that the crystals will have a new random number you will have to discover.
